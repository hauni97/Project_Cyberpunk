# Legal & Ethical Framework

This document outlines the legal boundaries and ethical guidelines for PROJECT CYBERPUNK usage.

---

## Legal Notice

**PROJECT CYBERPUNK is designed exclusively for authorized security assessments.**

Unauthorized use of this tool against systems you do not own or have explicit written permission to test is **illegal** and may result in criminal prosecution.

---

## 1. Legal Boundaries (Germany)

### Permitted (with restrictions)

- **Penetration testing** with written authorization
- **OSINT** on publicly accessible information
- **RF monitoring** of own frequencies
- **Security research** on own infrastructure

## 2. Ethical Use Framework

### 2.1 Red Team Operations

**Required Documentation:**

- **Scope of Work** clearly defined
- **Rules of Engagement** documented
- **Non-Disclosure Agreements** signed
- **Post-Engagement Reporting** provided
- **Data destruction** after project completion

### 2.2 OSINT Compliance

**Requirements:**

- No collection of personal data without legal basis
- **GDPR-compliant** data processing
- No surveillance without legal basis
- **Transparency** towards client

---

## 3. International Considerations

While this framework focuses on German law (StGB), users in other jurisdictions must comply with their local laws, including but not limited to:

- **USA:** Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA), wiretapping laws
- **UK:** Computer Misuse Act 1990
- **EU:** GDPR, national cybercrime laws

**Always consult with legal counsel before conducting security assessments.**

---

## 4. Responsible Disclosure

If vulnerabilities are discovered during authorized assessments:

1. **Notify the client immediately**
2. **Provide detailed documentation**
3. **Allow reasonable time for remediation**
4. **Do not publicly disclose** without client consent
5. **Follow coordinated disclosure** practices

---

## 5. User Responsibility

**Users of PROJECT CYBERPUNK are solely responsible for:**

- Obtaining proper authorization
- Complying with all applicable laws
- Ensuring ethical use
- Protecting collected data
- Proper disposal of sensitive information

**The project maintainers assume no liability for misuse.**
